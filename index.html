About Us

Hello, my name is Angelia Sjafri. I am a college student who aspires to help young students with their studies.

I started teaching when I was 15. I volunteered to teach students who couldn't afford the right education. I wanted to further my reach in helping struggling students with their education.

Experience

Over the past years, I have taught various students, both students from K-12 and College. I helped them go through obstacles in their studies, helped them understand the materials.


Programs

In Angelia's Learning Center, we offer MATH, SCIENCE, and ENGLISH tutoring. Any student that needs help with any subject offered can book an appointment through text or email provided below.
